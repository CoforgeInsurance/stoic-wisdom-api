# Content Quality Standards

This document outlines the standards for content quality within the Stoic Wisdom API.

## Quote Curation Criteria
- Ensure quotes are authentic and attributed to the correct philosopher.
- Provide context for each quote.

## Scientific Connections
- Explore connections to modern neuroscience, cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), and psychology.

## Source Attribution Requirements
- All content must include proper citations and sources.

## Content Review Process
- Establish a review process to ensure quality and accuracy of content.
